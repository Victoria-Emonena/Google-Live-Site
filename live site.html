What's New in Chrome

Get what you need from scanned PDFs

With Chrome, now you can search, select, or copy the text you want in scanned PDFs

1. When you come across a scanned PDF on a webpage, open it in chrome PDF viewer

2. Almost instantly, the PDF will become interactive.

3. Search, select, or copy the text you want

Search anything on your screen with Google Lens in Chrome

Draw, highlight, or tap to select anything you want to search and get results without leaving your tab.

iOS

1. Go to any web page in Chrome.

2. In the address bar, tap on the Google Lens camera icon.

3. Draw, highlight, or tap anything on your screen to search.

Android

You can use your Android device's Circle to Search feature to search what you see on Chrome as well as on any app on your Android device.

Scan the QR Code to get Chrome on your phone.

Share passwords on your phone

Easily and securely share a copy of a password with your family group in Google Password Manager on your phone.

iOS

1. Tap the Chrome menu > Password Manager.

2. Select the password you want to share, then tap Share .

3. Select family members to share your passwords with.

Android

1. Tap Chrome menu > Settings > Password Manager.

Under Search passwords, select the password you want to share, then tap Share

Select family members to share your password with.

Scan the QR Code to get Chrome on your phone.

A new way to customize your toolbar

Pin your favorite features and shortcuts as toolbar buttons.

1. At the top of your browser, select Chrome menu > More Tools > Customize Chrome

2. From the side panel, review the list of toolbar buttons that make it easy to quickly access things like bookmarks, print, Search with Google Lens, and more.

3. Choose the toolbar buttons you want to pin and they will appear in your toolbar.
